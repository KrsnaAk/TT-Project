Our Story

Established in 1998, SAVOR has been the premier destination for authentic vegetarian cuisine. We believe in the purity of ingredients, using farm-fresh vegetables and hand-ground spices to create dishes that comfort the soul.

Located in the heart of Bhilai, our warm ambiance and dedicated staff ensure a perfect dining experience for your family and friends.
